HOW IT WORKS

1

Apply Online

  • Submit photos and social links
  • Write personal statement
  • Complete application form
  • Deadline: October 5, 2025
2

Initial Screening

  • Our team reviews all applications
  • Selected ones are posted on Instagram
  • Public voting determines the Top 25
  • Winners compete at the main event
3

Contestants Confirmed

  • Top 25 from Instagram voting
  • Our team selects the other half
  • Selection based on brand alignment
  • Final contestants receive instructions
4

Main Event

  • Content shoots with all contestants
  • Rehearsal with every contestant
  • Compete in front of students and judges
  • Held at a local venue for ticket holders
